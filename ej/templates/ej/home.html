Ejub. From education to job.

What is Ejub?

From education to job. We try to dig into the link between the courses taught in school and the jobs to be filled in the industry using quantitave methods.

Bridge the gap between what education provides and what industry demands

How does it work?

Search for a class before you enroll. Ejub tells you what skills are taught in the class and what jobs are related to the skills.

Search for your ideal job to see what class you should take to prepare for it.

Prepare students early for their future.
